Welcome!

This website is dedicated to tracking my progress as a runner. Keeping track of all my miles and my times.

Running is my passion. Running is my life. Running is the fuel to my engine. Running is why I get up in the morning and running is why life can't seem to keep me down.

I improve every day I don't let the negative thoughts eat at me. I improve when I say, "today I'm going to be better". I hope that together we can all be better and that some how we can use this website to document it.
